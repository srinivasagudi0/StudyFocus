# StudyFocus 📚

**Your personal study companion that helps you build better learning habits.**

## What is StudyFocus?

StudyFocus is a simple yet powerful app designed to help you track your study sessions and understand your learning patterns. Whether you're preparing for exams, learning a new skill, or just want to be more productive, StudyFocus keeps you accountable and motivated.

## Why Use StudyFocus?

✨ **Track Your Progress** - Log every study session and see how much time you're investing in your goals

📊 **Get Insights** - Analyze your study patterns and discover what works best for you (Will implement in the future)

💪 **Stay Motivated** - Daily inspirational quotes to keep you focused and driven (Working on this feature)

🔐 **Your Data, Secure** - All your information stays on your device in a local database

## How It Works

1. **Sign Up** - Create your account in seconds
2. **Log Your Sessions** - Record each study session with topics and duration
3. **View Analytics** - See your study trends and habits at a glance
4. **Keep Learning** - Use insights to improve your study routine

## Getting Started

Simply log in, start tracking your study sessions, and watch your productivity soar! The app does the rest.

## Features

- 📝 Easy session logging
- 📈 Visual study analytics
- 🎯 Goal tracking
- 💡 Daily inspirational quotes
- 🔒 Secure local storage
